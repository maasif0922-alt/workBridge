WorkBridge reaches 50,000 Verified Workers!

Platform Update
February 27, 2026 By WorkBridge Team 5 min read

We are thrilled to share that WorkBridge has officially crossed the milestone of 50,000 verified workers across Pakistan and beyond! This achievement reflects the trust and hard work of our amazing community.

Our mission has always been to simplify the way workers find jobs and employers find reliable talent. With this new scale, we are introducing even better verification processes and faster interview bookings.

What this means for you?

For workers, this means more visibility and more diversified job categories. For employers, it means access to a larger pool of pre-vetted professionals ready to start work immediately.

Stay tuned as we roll out more features this month, including the new "Direct Chat" and "Instant Proposal" systems that will make hiring 10x faster.
